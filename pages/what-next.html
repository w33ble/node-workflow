Come see what other people are up to.

  • The NodeAZ Meetup is a great place to see some mind-blowing stuff being created with Node. It's also a great spot to catch some of the prolific Node developers in the valley.
  • The Phoenix Javascript Meetup isn't focused on Node, but as the name suggestions, it's all about Javascript. It usually covers frontend JS, but Node topics are commonly brought up as well.
  • The Arizona Software Community Meetup may not be Node or JS specific, but they come up often, and there's a lot of other great talks here too.
  • BeerJS PHX is also a great spot to meet other local JS developers, and grab a drink while you're at it.
